Spell: ice storm
Class: mage/sorc L4
Sphere: invocation_evocation
Saving throw: reflex
Syntax: cast <classname> ice storm

When the ice storm spell is cast, the mage summons storm clouds above that
rain hail and ice down upon anything in the area.  The spell will not
damage the caster and lasts for a number of rounds equal to the caster's
level. A versatile arcanist can manipulate the base element of this spell.

Components, mage/sorc: water x3, pinch of special dust x1 (or nil w. eschew
materials)

Spell: bestow curse
Class: mage L4
Sphere: necromancy
Saving throw: will
Syntax: cast <classname> bestow curse on <target>

By means of this spell, the caster places a curse upon the target that will
weaken them considerably, lowering their defenses and their combat
abilities. It is not dispellable by normal magics, but will fade over time.

Non-specialized mages cannot cast this spell.

Components, mage: charcoal x2 (or nil w. eschew materials)

Spell: animate dead
Class: cleric L3, mage/sorc L4
Sphere: necromancy
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> animate dead (on <target>)

This spell is pure evil, and only evil casters use it often. You can summon
a total number of hit dice worth of zombies and skeletons up to your
casting level.  They will follow you and defend you from your enemies. 
Unlike most common monster summoning spells, these monsters will not go
away unless the caster quits.  They are undead, so they can be turned, and
so forth, but they cannot be dispelled.

Components, mage/sorc: bit of bone x1, drop of blood x1 (or nil w. eschew
materials)

Spell: black tentacles
Class: mage L4
Sphere: conjuration_summoning
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> black tentacles

This spell summons up a mass of writhing black tentacles from the earth at
your feet. These will ensnare anything nearby to slow their escape, and
attempt to knock them from their feet.

Non-specialized mages cannot cast this spell.

Components, mage: carbon x1 (or nil w. eschew materials)

Spell: water breathing
Class: mage/sorc L4
Sphere: alteration
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> vacuum breathing on <target>

This spell allows the target to survive in a vacuum for a limited period of
time.

Components, mage/sorc: bell jar x1 (or nil w. eschew materials)

Spell: fire shield
Class: cleric L3, mage/sorc L4
Sphere: invocation_evocation
Domain: fire
Saving throw: fort
Syntax: cast <classname> fire shield

This spell will surround the caster's body in a roaring shield of flames. 
It grants the caster a slight bonus to their armor class, and any opponent
striking the caster must make a saving throw or take damage from the
blistering heat.

Non-specialized mages cannot cast this spell.

Components, mage: phosphorus x1 (or nil w. eschew materials)
