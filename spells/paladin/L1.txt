Spell: bolster
Class: paladin L1
Sphere: alteration
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> bolster on <target>

This spell will bolster the target, giving them a slightly better chance to
hit in combat as well as allowing them to do a small amount more damage.


Spell: detect chaos
Class: cleric L1, paladin L1
Sphere: divination
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> detect chaos on <target> || <room>

Detect chaos will detect the presence of chaos in players or monsters.
