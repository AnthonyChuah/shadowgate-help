Spell: create food
Class: cleric L3, paladin L2
Sphere: conjuration_summoning
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> create food

This spell creates a pile of edible food. You can eat some if you wish. The
higher level the caster, the more food will be created.


Spell: fire shield
Class: cleric L3, mage/sorc L4
Sphere: invocation_evocation
Domain: fire
Saving throw: fort
Syntax: cast <classname> fire shield

This spell will surround the caster's body in a roaring shield of flames. 
It grants the caster a slight bonus to their armor class, and any opponent
striking the caster must make a saving throw or take damage from the
blistering heat.

Non-specialized mages cannot cast this spell.

Components, mage: phosphorus x1 (or nil w. eschew materials)

Class: cleric L3, druid L3
Sphere: weather
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> call lightning on <target>

This is a destructive spell that could hurt an opponent very badly!  The
higher level the caster is the more devastating this spell will become. 
The spell can now be used anywhere, regardless of weather or if the caster
is indoors.

Spell: dispel magic
Class: cleric L3, paladin L3, druid L4, mage/sorc L3, bard L3
Sphere: abjuration
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> dispel magic (on <target>)

Dispel Magic, when cast, will destroy any spell enchantments, any magical
summonings, and anything conjured.  The chances of each dispelling depends
on both the dispeller's casting level and the casting level of the wizard
or priest that created whatever is being dispelled, whether it be an
enchantment, summoning, or conjuration.


Spell: animate dead
Class: cleric L3, mage/sorc L4
Sphere: necromancy
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> animate dead (on <target>)

This spell is pure evil, and only evil casters use it often. You can summon
a total number of hit dice worth of zombies and skeletons up to your
casting level.  They will follow you and defend you from your enemies. 
Unlike most common monster summoning spells, these monsters will not go
away unless the caster quits.  They are undead, so they can be turned, and
so forth, but they cannot be dispelled.

Components, mage/sorc: bit of bone x1, drop of blood x1 (or nil w. eschew
materials)
