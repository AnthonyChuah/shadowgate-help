Spell: burning hands
Class: mage/sorc L1, monk L3
Sphere: alteration
Saving throw: reflex
Syntax: cast <classname> burning hands on <target>

This spell begins by the caster fanning out his hands while chanting the
spell.  Flames will flare out and do a small amount of damage to the target
and those near him. A versatile arcanist can manipulate the base element of
this spell.


Spell: darkness
Class: cleric L2, mage/sorc L2, monk L3, bard L2
Sphere: alteration
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> darkness on <target>

This will create a globe of darkness, which will overcome normal light
sources. You can cast it on an object or player which it will follow,
instead of the caster.

Spell: ghost step
Class: cleric L2, ranger L1, monk L3
Sphere: alteration
Domain: animal
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> ghost step

This spell allows you to become one with nature, as a ghost of the forest,
leaving no traces of where you have walked.


Spell: darkvision
Class: ranger L3, mage/sorc L2, monk L3
Sphere: alteration
Saving throw: n/a
Syntax: cast <classname> darkvision

This spell will enhance the vision of the caster, allowing them better
sight in areas of extreme light or darkness.

Non-specialized mages cannot cast this spell.
